About

I'm Vineeth, but most folks online know me as TMK. I'm a creative who loves translating everyday moments into punchy, story-driven videos. I lean into playful energy, quick rhythm, and structure, always trying to make people feel emotion without wasting their time.

Tools I use: InShot for short-form and non-traditional content, Photoshop for graphic design, Premiere Pro for more formal showcases, and the usual creator stack for social.
